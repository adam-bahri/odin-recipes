Tiramisu

Home

Ingredients

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 cup mascarpone cheese
  • 1 cup strong brewed coffee, cooled
  • 2 tablespoons coffee liqueur
  • 24 ladyfingers
  • 1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder

Instructions

  1. In a medium bowl, beat heavy cream and sugar with an electric mixer until stiff peaks form.
  2. Add mascarpone to whipped cream and beat until well combined.
  3. In a small shallow dish, add coffee and coffee liqueur. Dip each ladyfinger into coffee mixture, soaking them for a few seconds.
  4. Arrange a layer of soaked ladyfingers in the bottom of a 7x11 inch dish.
  5. Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers, then repeat with remaining ladyfingers and mascarpone mixture.
  6. Cover and refrigerate 4 to 6 hours, until set.
  7. Dust with cocoa before serving.
