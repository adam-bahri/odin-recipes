Pizza

Home

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup sliced olives
  • 1/4 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup sliced bell peppers

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce.
  5. Add the pepperoni, olives, mushrooms, and bell peppers.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown.
  7. Remove from the oven and let cool for a few minutes before slicing.
